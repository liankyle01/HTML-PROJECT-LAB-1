<h1 align="center">5 Seconds of Summer</h1>
<center>
<p><b>5 Seconds of Summer</b> (5SOS) is an Australian pop-rock band formed in 2011. <p>
The band gained international fame after touring with One Direction and releasing multiple hit albums. </p>
<p>They have sold millions of records worldwide and are known for their energetic performances and diverse musical style.</p>
</center>

<h2 align="center">Band Members</h2>
<div align="center">
    <img src="band.jpg" alt="5SOS Band Members" width="600" height="380">
</div>
<ul>
    <li><b>Luke Hemmings</b> - Lead vocals, rhythm guitar</li>
    <li><b>Michael Clifford</b> - Lead guitar, vocals</li>
    <li><b>Calum Hood</b> - Bass guitar, vocals</li>
    <li><b>Ashton Irwin</b> - Drums, vocals</li>

</ul>

<h2>Achievements</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Over 10 million albums sold worldwide</li>
    <li>Billboard Music Award for Top New Artist (2015)</li>
    <li>MTV Video Music Award for Best Song of the Summer</li>
    <li>ARIA Awards for Best Group multiple times</li>
    <li>Multiple platinum-certified albums and singles</li>
</ul>

<h2>Fun Facts</h2>
<ul>
    <li>5SOS started as YouTube cover artists before gaining global recognition.</li>
    <li>They have collaborated with artists like The Chainsmokers and Julia Michaels.</li>
    <li>Each band member contributes to songwriting and production.</li>
    <li>The band's name was inspired by a random brainstorming session before their first gig.</li>
</ul>
